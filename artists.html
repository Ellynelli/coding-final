Jen Zee is Supergiant Game's art director. In the company's first game, Bastion, Zee created all the 2D art â€“ from the environments to the ui and item icons. When Supergiant began hiring more help and new artists for their second game, Transistor, Zee became the art director. While she no longer creates every asset herself, she leads the team and establishes an artistic vision for each project. She plays a large part in the company's concept art and her work is often featured in character merchandise.

Zee has expressed a love for color that shines through in each game's vivid pallets. From desaturated hues to neon lights, Zee's influence is evident.
